Report

Working on the DetectiBot project was a valuable learning experience for our team. We were able to apply theoretical knowledge from Health Sciences and combine it with technology to solve a real-world problem related to patient safety.

During the development of the prototype, we learned how to connect electronic components, program the Arduino Uno, and test the system in different scenarios. At first, we faced some difficulties with sensor calibration and wiring connections, but through teamwork and problem-solving, we were able to improve the system’s performance.

This project helped us understand the importance of interdisciplinary collaboration between healthcare and technology. It also showed us that low-cost technological solutions can make a meaningful difference in hospital safety and patient monitoring.

Overall, this experience strengthened our teamwork, creativity, and technical skills, and motivated us to continue exploring innovative solutions in the healthcare field.
